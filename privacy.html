Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

Overview

BirdID Lite is a simple quiz app. We do not sell your data or use third-party advertising trackers.

What we collect

  • Basic usage metrics (optional): Anonymous counters like number of quiz plays and correct answers, stored locally in your browser (localStorage) for your personal stats.
  • Donations: If you donate via Pi Browser, the Pi SDK processes the payment. We may record a public, non-identifying donation entry (amount, date, optional memo) in our public ledger.
  • No account required: We don’t require login for taking the quiz.

Cookies & local storage

We may use localStorage for your quiz stats. You can clear it anytime via your browser settings.

Third-party services

  • Pi Browser / Pi SDK: Used only to initiate voluntary donations when you choose to tip. Outside the Pi Browser, the donation button is disabled or shows guidance.
  • Hosting/CDN: Static files are served via Cloudflare Pages. They may collect standard logs for security and performance.

Data retention

Public donation entries (amount/date/memo) remain in the Public Donations Ledger. No personal identifiers are published.

Your choices

  • You can use the quiz without donating.
  • You can clear local stats via your browser.
